Organization

  • We start with 10 min overview presentations in the first 2 class meetings.
  • Each student should email me a summary of the status of their project and their expected progress by the end of the semester.
  • Grades will be assigned on the basis of presentations and course participation.

Presentation guidelines

  • After the short presentations each student presents 1 project per week.
  • Presenters must circulate drafts 1 week prior.
  • I expect everyone to have read the paper and to participate in the discussion.
  • Those at an early stage (without a draft paper) should prepare presentations that focus on:
    • What is the question?
    • Why is it interesting?
    • What is the approach to be taken?
    • How does it contribute to the literature?
    • What is the expected punchline?
  • Presentations should downplay technical issues / details.
  • The first slide of each presentation should say the following:
    • Faculty you are working with
    • Status (e.g., "rough draft")
    • Question: 1 sentence, ending with "?"
  • After each presentation I will summarize the main open issues that should be worked on. The next presentation should focus on the progress made on those issues.
